About Me ([CV] [Google Scholar] [GitHub] [LinkedIn])

Currently, I am a final year PhD student in Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Northwestern University, advised by Prof. Ying Wu. Before joining Northwestern, I received my B.S degree in Mathematics Elite Class, M.S degree in Statistics, both from Xi'an Jiaotong University, advised by Prof. Zongben Xu and Prof. Deyu Meng.

I have broad research interests in the areas of computer vision and machine learning, including:

  • Adversarial robustness (current).
  • Few-shot learning and meta learning.
  • 2D/3D human pose estimation.
  • Low-level vision, image/video restoration.
