About Me [Curriculum Vitae] [GitHub] [LinkedIn]

Currently, I am a Ph.D. candidate in Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Northwestern University, advised by Prof. Ying Wu.

I have broad research interests in the fields of machine learning and computer vision. My current projects include:


  • Trustworthy Machine Learning: Adversarial Robustness, Out-of-Distribution Generalization
  • Probabilistic methods: Bayesian methods, Variational Inference, Uncertainty Quantification

Some past research projects include:


  • General Machine Learning: Few-Shot Learning, Meta-Learning, Continual Learning
  • Image Processing and Low-Level Vision: Visual Quality Enhancement under Unsatisfactory Imagery
  • Visual Sensing and Understanding: 2D/3D Human Pose Estimation, Object Detection
